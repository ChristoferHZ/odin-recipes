Descriptions

Crispy baked pizza

  • 1 pre-made pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup of tomato sauce
  • 1 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup of crumbled blue cheese
  • 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup of shredded provolone cheese
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish (optional)

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C). If you're using a pizza stone, place it in the oven to heat up as well.
  2. Sauté the Aromatics: Add the chopped onion and garlic to the pot. Sauté for about 5 minutes until the onion is translucent and the garlic is fragrant.
  3. Add the Sauce: Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the pizza dough, leaving a small border around the edges for the crust.
  4. Add the Cheeses: Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese evenly over the tomato sauce. Next, add the crumbled blue cheese, grated Parmesan cheese, and shredded provolone cheese.
  5. Add the Broth: Pour in the vegetable broth, making sure all the vegetables are submerged. Bring the mixture to a boil.
  6. Season: Drizzle the olive oil over the top of the pizza and sprinkle with dried oregano.
  7. Bake the Pizza: Carefully transfer the pizza to the preheated oven (or onto the pizza stone if using). Bake for 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheeses are melted and bubbly.
  8. Garnish and Serve: Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a couple of minutes. Garnish with fresh basil leaves if desired. Slice and serve hot.
