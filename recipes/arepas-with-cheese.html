Crispy arepas with cheese

  • 2 cups of pre-cooked cornmeal (such as P.A.N. or Masarepa)
  • 2 1/2 cups of warm water
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil or butter (optional)
  • Additional oil or butter for cooking

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Heat the Oil: In a large pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat.
  2. Sauté the Aromatics: Add the chopped onion and garlic to the pot. Sauté for about 5 minutes until the onion is translucent and the garlic is fragrant.
  3. Add the Vegetables: Add the carrots, celery, potatoes, zucchini, and broccoli to the pot. Stir well to combine with the onions and garlic.
  4. Cook the Vegetables: Sauté the vegetables for about 10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until they start to soften.
  5. Add the Broth: Pour in the vegetable broth, making sure all the vegetables are submerged. Bring the mixture to a boil.
  6. Simmer: Reduce the heat to low and let the soup simmer for about 20-25 minutes, or until all the vegetables are tender.
  7. Blend the Soup: Use an immersion blender to puree the soup directly in the pot until smooth. Alternatively, you can transfer the soup in batches to a blender and blend until smooth. Be careful with the hot liquid.
  8. Add the Cream: Return the pureed soup to the pot if you used a blender. Stir in the heavy cream until well combined.
  9. Season: Taste the soup and add salt and pepper as needed.
  10. Garnish and Serve: Ladle the hot soup into bowls. Garnish with fresh herbs like parsley or thyme. Serve immediately.
